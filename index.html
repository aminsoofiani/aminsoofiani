Amin Soofiani

Welcome to my homepage!

I completed my PhD in mathematics at the University of British Columbia in 2025 under the supervision of Sujatha Ramdorai and Vladimir Chernousov.

From 2020 to 2024, I was a sessional instructor of Mathematics at Columbia College, Vancouver, Canada.
From 2024 to 2025, I worked at Columbia College as a gridded (semi-permanent) instructor of Mathematics.

Research interests: linear algebraic groups, Galois cohomology, quadratic forms.

Email: soofiani@math.ubc.ca
