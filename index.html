Hello — I’m Amin Soufiani. I completed my PhD in Mathematics at UBC in Summer 2025 under the supervision of Vladimir Chernousov and Sujatha Ramdorai. My thesis was titled “Hensel’s Lemma for the Norm Principle for Type Dn Groups.” Before that, I completed my MSc (2019) also at UBC under Michael Bennett and Sujatha Ramdorai.

Research Interests

I work in the areas of linear algebraic groups, Galois cohomology, and quadratic / Hermitian forms. I’m especially interested in questions around norms, cohomological invariants, and structural behavior of algebraic groups.

Outside of research, I teach mathematics at Columbia College in Vancouver. From 2021 to 2024, I served as Director of the Mathematics Learning Centre (MLC) at UBC.
